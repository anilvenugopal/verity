J1

From sign-up to first article

10 stops · ~5 min real time
The new author's first session

Verification happens before any setup. The 3 welcome modals overlay an empty dashboard — each is skippable. After dismissal, a soft toast handles deferred setup (e.g. style training). Sign-up never collects a credit card; Free users bring their own API key.

  S -->|email + password| V("Verify emailpage")

  W2 -->|next| W3("Welcome 3 · Style CTAmodal")

  W2 -.->|skip| D
  W3 -.->|skip · toast reminds later| D
  W3 -->|train now| ST("Style training uploadpage")
  ST --> D
  D -->|New article| K("Add AI keyFree tier · page")

J2

The editor journey

6 phases · forks & branches
From a blank canvas to a published article

The 6 phases are the spine. Authors can fork branches, switch templates mid-article, or roll back via checkpoints at any point. The editor itself is one canvas; phases change which panels appear and how the AI behaves.

flowchart LR
  S("Stormpage") -->|promote angle| D("Draftpage")
  D -->|sections complete| C("Curatepage")
  C -->|reviews resolved| P("Polishpage")
  P -->|voice match passes| PR("Proofpage")
  PR -->|originality + citations OK| PB("Publishpage")
  C -.->|fork an alternate| BR("Branch switchermodal")
  BR -.->|new branch| D
  C -.->|switch template| TS("Switch templatemodal")
  TS --> C
  P -.->|compare versions| VP("Version pickermodal")
  VP --> P
  PR -.->|originality check| OR("Originality resultspage")
  OR --> PR
  PB -->|push to CMS| EX("Exportmodal")

J3

Bringing a reviewer in

Collaboration loop
Round-based feedback with AI synthesis

Reviewers operate in a read-only mode and leave proposals. The author resolves them and AI synthesises themes when the round closes. Co-authors (Team tier) can push direct edits via the acceptance modal.

flowchart LR
  C("Curate phasepage") -->|open Review panel| RP("Review panelcomponent")
  RP -->|invite| RI("Reviewer invitemodal")
  RI -->|email sent| RM("Reviewer modepage")
  RM -->|comment · suggest| RP2("Review panel updatescomponent")
  RP2 -->|close round| RS("Round summarymodal")
  RS -->|next round| RP
  RS -->|approve all| P("Polish phasepage")
  RM -.->|co-author push| CA("Co-author acceptancemodal")
  CA --> RP2
  RP -.->|manage roles| PM("Permissionsmodal")
  PM --> RP

J4

Style training & template creation

Authoring constraints
Teach the studio your voice and structure

Two parallel learning surfaces: voice (style training) and structure (custom templates). Both feed the AI Director's context. Templates can carry per-section AI directives on Pro+ plans.

Account, billing & team flows

Settings navigation
Where the avatar menu leads

Every settings destination is one click from the avatar menu (top-right of any page) or the workspace menu (≡, top-left). Free-tier users use BYOK; Pro/Team get managed AI with credits.
