Roles & Permissions

Who does what in the Verity AI governance platform.

Platform roles

AI Governance
Reviews and approves application onboarding and intake submissions. The primary governance authority.
Business Owner
Accountable for an application and its use cases. Submits onboarding requests and intakes for approval.
Compliance
Signs off on compliance-sensitive intakes. Approves or rejects compliance exceptions.
Model Risk
Reviews model risk aspects of high-tier intakes.
Security
Reviews security aspects. Co-approves compliance exceptions.
Legal
Reviews legal and regulatory exposure for high-tier intakes.
Privacy
Reviews privacy and data protection aspects for intakes involving PII/PHI.

Separation of duty

The person who submits a request cannot approve it. This applies to intake approvals, change proposals, and compliance exceptions.
