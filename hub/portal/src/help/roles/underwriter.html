Underwriter

Assess and approve AI use cases affecting insurance decisions.

Responsibilities

The Underwriter role is responsible for evaluating AI systems that support or make insurance decisions — underwriting, pricing, claims, fraud detection, and eligibility. Underwriters review intake assessments to ensure the risk tier, data sourcing, and human oversight controls are appropriate for the nature and scale of the decision.

What an Underwriter can do

  • Create, edit, and submit intakes (as intake owner)
  • Complete and save risk assessments
  • Review and sign off on approval requests as a quorum member
  • Record evidence against compliance obligations
  • Raise exceptions (subject to Compliance Officer or Risk Manager approval)
  • Raise change proposals
  • Create and manage registry assets
  • View all applications, intakes, and registry assets

Separation of duty

An Underwriter cannot approve an intake or change proposal they submitted. This prevents a single actor from both proposing and approving a governance decision. If you are the submitter, a colleague with the Underwriter, Compliance Officer, or Risk Manager role must provide the sign-off.
