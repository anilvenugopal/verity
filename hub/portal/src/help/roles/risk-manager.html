Risk Manager

Oversee risk tier decisions and approve high-risk exceptions.

Responsibilities

The Risk Manager role oversees the risk assessment process and ensures appropriate controls are in place for AI systems operating at higher tiers. Risk Managers sign off on intakes where the tier requires their review, and approve exceptions for controls that carry significant residual risk.

What a Risk Manager can do

  • Review and sign off on approval requests as a quorum member
  • Approve or reject exception requests (compensating-control waivers)
  • Review change proposals — especially risk reclassifications that alter the tier
  • Browse the compliance model to understand control requirements at each tier
  • View all applications, intakes, and registry assets

Tier-based quorum

Risk Manager sign-off is required for intakes assessed at higher risk tiers (Tier 3 and above in the default configuration). Lower-tier intakes may only require Underwriter and Compliance Officer sign-off. The exact quorum configuration is determined by the compliance metamodel seeded for your organisation.

Separation of duty

A Risk Manager cannot approve an intake or change proposal they submitted. This applies regardless of tier.
