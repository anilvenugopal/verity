Compliance Officer

Ensure AI systems meet regulatory obligations and approve exceptions.

Responsibilities

The Compliance Officer role is responsible for ensuring AI systems satisfy the regulatory obligations that apply at their risk tier. Compliance Officers review evidence, approve or reject exceptions where standard controls cannot be met, and sign off on intakes that require compliance-role quorum approval.

What a Compliance Officer can do

  • Review and sign off on approval requests as a quorum member
  • Approve or reject exception requests (compensating-control waivers)
  • Review obligation evidence submitted by intake owners
  • Review and sign off on change proposals
  • Browse the compliance model (canonical requirements, controls, evidence specs)
  • View all applications, intakes, and registry assets

Separation of duty

A Compliance Officer cannot approve an intake or change proposal they submitted. Exception approvals do not require a separate submitter — the Compliance Officer approves exceptions raised by any team member, including Underwriters.
