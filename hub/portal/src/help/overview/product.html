About Verity

AI governance for regulated insurance — what the platform does and why it exists.

What Verity governs

Verity is an AI governance platform for insurance carriers operating under regulations that require documented, auditable oversight of AI systems: the EU AI Act, the NAIC Model Bulletin on AI, NY DFS Circular Letter 7, and Colorado SB21-169, among others.

Every AI system that makes or supports a consequential insurance decision — underwriting, pricing, claims, fraud detection, eligibility — must be registered, assessed, and approved before it can go to production.

The governance lifecycle

An application represents an AI system or product. Under each application, intakes represent individual use cases. Each intake goes through an assessment (five-section risk analysis), approval (quorum sign-off), and obligation resolution (satisfying compliance controls) before its registry assets can be promoted to production.
