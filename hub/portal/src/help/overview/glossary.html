Glossary

Key terms used in the Verity platform.

Application
The organisational container for AI use cases. Must be approved before intakes can be created under it.
Intake
A governed AI use case. Goes through assessment, approval, and obligation resolution before its assets can go to production.
Risk tier
The computed governance classification: Limited, High, or Unacceptable. Determined by the assessment and drives the approver quorum and obligation set.
Obligation
A compliance requirement resolved from the metamodel based on the intake's risk tier and governance domains. Must be satisfied (evidence recorded) or excepted before production promotion.
Registry asset
A governed AI model, agent, or task tracked through the lifecycle from draft to production.
Promotion gate
The mechanism that blocks a registry asset from advancing to challenger or champion unless its linked intake is approved with all obligations resolved.
Change proposal
A formal request to modify an approved intake's risk profile or business scope. Triggers re-approval and, for risk reclassifications, re-resolution of obligations.
Quorum
The set of approver roles that must sign off before a request is approved. Determined by the intake's risk tier.
