Intake Approval Workflow

The end-to-end process for getting a use case approved and into production.

Steps

  1. Create intake — propose the use case under an active application.
  2. Complete assessment — fill all five sections; the system computes the risk tier on save.
  3. Submit for approval — opens a quorum request; the tier determines which roles must sign off.
  4. Quorum sign-off — required approvers review and sign. Separation of duty: the submitter cannot approve their own request.
  5. Approved — obligations are resolved; registry assets can advance to production.
