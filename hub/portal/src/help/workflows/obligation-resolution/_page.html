Obligation Resolution

Satisfying the compliance obligations that gate production deployment.

Steps

  1. Review — after intake approval, obligations are resolved from the metamodel based on risk tier and governance domains.
  2. Record evidence — for each control, record evidence of implementation. All controls evidenced = obligation satisfied.
  3. Raise exception — for obligations that cannot be satisfied, raise a time-limited exception. Requires approver sign-off (separation of duty).
  4. Resolved — all obligations satisfied or excepted. Registry assets can now be promoted to production.
