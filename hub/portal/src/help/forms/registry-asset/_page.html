Registry Asset

A governed AI model, agent, or task tracked through the lifecycle from draft to production.

Purpose

The registry is the authoritative list of AI executables under governance. Each asset has one or more versions; each version independently advances through the lifecycle stages. Promotion to a production stage (challenger or champion) requires a linked intake that is approved and has all obligations resolved.

Lifecycle stages

Draft → Candidate → Staging
Pre-governance stages. No intake required.
Challenger / Champion
Production stages. Blocked by the promotion gate until the linked intake is approved and obligations are resolved.
Deprecated
Terminal stage. No further advancement.
