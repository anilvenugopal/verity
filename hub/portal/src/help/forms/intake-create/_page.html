Create Intake

Register a new AI use case for governance review under a governed application.

Purpose

An intake is the governed record of a single AI use case. It captures what the system decides, over whom, and with what risk profile. Every AI use case that touches a regulated decision must have an approved intake before it can go to production.

Fields

Use case title
A short, descriptive name. Appears in approval queues and reporting.
Description
What the AI system does and how it is used in business context.
Application
The parent application. Must be active (onboarding approved). Sets the regulatory and jurisdictional context for this intake.

What happens next

After creation, complete the AI Decision Impact Assessment. Once a risk tier is computed, submit for approval. The tier determines the approver quorum.
