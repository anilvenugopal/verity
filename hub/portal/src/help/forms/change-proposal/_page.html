Change Proposal

Propose a material change to an approved intake's risk profile or business operation.

Purpose

An approved intake is a governed record. Any material change — a new data source, a changed decision scope, a new jurisdiction — must be documented and re-approved through a change proposal. This preserves the audit trail and ensures obligations are re-evaluated.

Proposal kinds

Risk reclassification
Re-runs the tier computation. If the tier changes, obligations are re-resolved and impacted assets are forked to a new draft on approval.
Business change
Documents a material operational change. The tier is not recomputed unless explicitly requested.

What happens next

The proposal enters the approval queue with the same quorum as the original intake. On approval, impacted registry assets are forked and must re-advance through the lifecycle.
